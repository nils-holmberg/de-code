About the workshop

de-code is a practical workshop for people who want to use AI-assisted workflows for computational work. Participants learn how to translate questions into analysis, prompts into datasets, and ideas into lightweight applications.

The workshop combines short lectures, guided exercises, and a practical mini-project.

Five-day schedule

Day 1

Foundations: Thinking in AI-assisted workflows

Introduction to AI-assisted coding, low-code paradigms, prompt-based workflows, and turning questions into executable steps.

Day 2

de-code data analysis

Clean, structure, analyze, visualize, and report data using natural language interfaces and AI-supported analytical workflows.

Day 3

de-code data generation

Generate synthetic datasets, simulations, test cases, and teaching examples from structured descriptions and prompts.

Day 4

de-code app building

Build lightweight dashboards, prototypes, forms, and internal tools by connecting data, logic, and user interaction.

Day 5

Integration and application

Combine analysis, generation, and app building into an end-to-end AI-assisted workflow, followed by mini-project presentations.

Learning outcomes

After the workshop, participants will be able to:

Translate problems into AI-assisted workflows
Analyze and visualize data using natural language
Generate synthetic datasets for testing and teaching
Build simple functional applications and prototypes

Workshop format

Short lectures

Compact conceptual introductions to AI-assisted coding, data analysis, generation, and app building.

Guided hands-on sessions

Step-by-step exercises where participants turn natural language instructions into working computational outputs.

Mini-project

A small applied project where participants design and present their own de-code workflow.

Start with a question

Bring your data, idea, or workflow problem. Leave with a practical AI-assisted method for turning it into analysis, data, or an app.
